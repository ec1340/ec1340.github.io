### A Little About Me

I spend a lot of my time these days researching and building agentic systems and trying to understand scientific reasoning.


I completed my PhD in Computational Biology and Bioinformatics at Yale University in [Dr.Smita Krishnaswamy's lab](https://www.krishnaswamylab.org/). I enjoy thinking across disciplines and learning how machine learning can improve our understanding of complex biological systems.

Before graduate school, I interned at Genentech applying machine learning to internal chemical data for property prediction. I majored in Pharmacological Chemistry with a minor in Mathematics at UC San Diego, where I conducted research in the [Amaro Lab](https://amarolab.ucsd.edu/) and [Walker Lab](http://www.rosswalker.co.uk).
